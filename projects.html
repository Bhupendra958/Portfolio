Parent Meeting Portal

The Parent Meeting Portal is a web-based application designed to simplify communication between parents and teachers by enabling structured meeting scheduling and real-time interaction. The system improves school management by providing a centralized platform for appointment booking, meeting tracking, and efficient communication.

  • Online parent-teacher meeting scheduling system.
  • Real-time meeting slot management.
  • User-friendly dashboard for parents and teachers.
  • Efficient communication and appointment tracking.
  • Responsive design for all devices.

MedQueue – Digital Queue System for Hospitals

MedQueue is a web-based digital queue management system designed to reduce hospital waiting times by enabling online appointment booking and real-time token generation. The platform improves hospital workflow efficiency by digitizing queue handling and providing a structured appointment management system.

  • Online doctor appointment booking with automated token generation.
  • Department-wise doctor filtering system.
  • Admin panel for walk-in registrations and queue monitoring.
  • User dashboard showing appointment details and token tracking.
  • Responsive UI optimized for mobile and desktop.

CloverEduForKids – Interactive Learning Platform

CloverEduForKids is an interactive educational website designed to make learning engaging and accessible for children through quizzes, stories, and subject-based learning modules. The platform focuses on combining education with interactivity to improve curiosity, logical thinking, and academic skills.

  • Subject-based learning modules including Math, Science, and English.
  • Interactive quiz system with timers and multiple difficulty levels.
  • Moral story section promoting positive thinking.
  • Educational video integration for visual learning.
  • Responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
